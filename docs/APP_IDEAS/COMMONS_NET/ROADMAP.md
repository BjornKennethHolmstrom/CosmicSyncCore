#### Pilot 1: Urban Neighborhood (Stockholm)
- **Focus**: Tool sharing, skill exchange, local services
- **Partners**: Local community organizations, housing cooperatives
- **Metrics**: Resource utilization, social connection, economic savings

- **Focus**: Agricultural equipment, seasonal labor, local produce
